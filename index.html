Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions

How much does laundry service cost?

Basic wash & fold service is ₡2,500 per kilogram. Special items and add-on services have their own pricing. See our pricing section for complete details.

What areas do you service?

We provide free pickup and delivery throughout Tamarindo, Costa Rica. If you're outside this area, please contact us to discuss options.

How long does laundry take?

Standard turnaround is 3-5 hours. Express service may be available upon request. Commercial clients can arrange daily pickups.

Do you handle special items?

Yes! We specialize in sneakers, quilts, curtains, carpets, pillows and more. Each item has specific care protocols to ensure quality results.

What add-on services do you offer?

We offer OxyClean, Whitener, and Vanish treatments to enhance cleaning results for ₡1,000 each.

Is delivery really free?

Yes! Free pickup and delivery throughout Tamarindo with no hidden charges. That's part of our commitment to convenient service.
